Welcome to the migraine tracking and visualising web app

Let's get you set up

 
        To get the best results from this app track your migraine data daily to look for patterns and to identify triggers.
        
        Add your data through the side bar button : Add Data 
        View a history of all your tracked data through the Logged Data button 

        The more data you track the more information will be added to your graphs for the month. Use your graphs to look for what
        could be causing your migraines to get worse and learn more about your health.

        You can edit your data, but only one set of data is saved for the day. You will overwrite any old data. You can backtrack your
        data so you can log data at a time that suits you.

        The home page is where you can view a summary of all your data, this data has been visualised for you to make it easier to read.
        Click on the title of each graph from the home page to view in-depth detail of this tracker.

        Click on a graph key (coloured box) to hide that element of the graph.
